Buckhorn Lake (aka Buckhorn Reservoir)

Buckhorn Lake is a 2,000 acre lake in the east-central part of the state, 10 miles north of Kenly. Buckhorn Lake is the largest fishing spot near the city, covering more than 2,100 acres of water and land. You can fish from docks, shore or boat. The depth of the reservoir is around 25 feet at maximum, water clarity is 6-10 feet. Fishermen report catching bream, bluegill, crappie, largemouth bass, muskie, white bass, catfish and rainbow trout.
