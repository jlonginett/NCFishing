Muskie

Muskie are the largest of the pike family, they are found in about half the states. Ideal water temperature: 55° to 70°. Muskie are found primarily the northern and northeastern states. They prefer clear water lakes and streams. Also known as musky, muskie and great pike, they eat any fish they can catch and prefer to ambush their prey. They also eat crayfish, frogs and birds.Use large lures (salt-water sizes) and heavy line when fishing for muskellunge. They are known for their speed and are exceptionally strong for short runs. They will hit large musky lures and fresh cut bait.

List of Best Lures and Bait to use:

Muskie primarily eat fish, but their diet also consists of many other food sources. Frogs, birds, crustaceans and most anything that moves in, or on the water is subject to being eaten by these large predators. Big lures, bright colors, flash, vibration and commotion peak their interest. If they think it is edible, they will most likely attack. These pike have exceptionally sharp teeth, so for whatever lure you choose, use a wire leader.

BEST LURES:

TOPWATER LURES

  • Effective depth: surface
  • Recommended line test: 14-17 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use topwater lures when muskie are relatively shallow and fairly aggressive.

TOPWATER POPPERS

  • Effective depth: surface
  • Recommended line test: 14-17 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use topwater poppers when muskie are relatively shallow and need a little coaxing.

Spinners

INLINE SPINNERS

  • Effective depth: 2' to 10'
  • Recommended line test: 10-15 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use inline spinners when muskie are shallow or just outside weedlines.

Spoons

FLUTTER SPOONS

  • Effective depth: 2' to 60'
  • Recommended line test: 10-15 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use spoons in all depths and fishing conditions.

JIGGING SPOONS

  • Effective depth: 8' to 60'
  • Recommended line test: 14-17 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use spoons in all depths and fishing conditions.

Crankbaits

LIPLESS CRANKBAITS

  • Effective depth: 2' to 10'
  • Recommended line test: 10-15 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use large lipless crankbaits for fishing shallow to mid depths.

MEDUIM DEPTH CRANKBAITS

  • Effective depth: 5' to 10' or more
  • Recommended line test: 14-17 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use medium depth crankbaits for fishing open water around rocky or muddy bottoms.

DEEP DIVING CRANKBAITS

  • Effective depth: 5' to 20' or more
  • Recommended line test: 14-17 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use deep diving crankbaits for fishing deeper ledges, creek channels and structure.

Jerkbaits

JERKBAITS & RIPBAITS

  • Effective depth: 2' to 8'
  • Recommended line test: 10-15 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use large jerkbaits or rip baits for fishing shallow to mid depths.

Swimbaits

SOFT SWIMBAITS

  • Effective depth: 2' to 10'
  • Recommended line test: 10-15 pound
  • Ideal conditions: Use swimbaits for fishing open water in shallow to mid depths.
