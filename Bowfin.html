Bowfin

The bowfin is also known as dogfish, mud pike, mudfish, grindle and swamp bass. Ideal water temperature: 68° to 82°. They are usually found in waters around Alabama, Florida, Louisiana and Mississippi, the bowfin prefers water temperatures of the warmer climates. It has an exceptionally long dorsal fin which extends back to the tail, made of about 50 spines or rays. Near the upper tail is an eye spot. They have very sharp teeth and a nasty disposition when hooked. Handle with caution. They put up quite a fight and are not considered good eating.

List of Best Lures and Bait to use:

They are most often caught on cut bait and crayfish.
