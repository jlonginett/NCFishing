Lake Cammack

Lake Cammack is an 850 acre reservoir that is 5 miles long with a shoreline of over 50 miles. The park that serves as the Marina consists of 62 acres of rolling land. The facility has many opportunites for the public. Features of the Lake Include: Boating Bank Fishing Skiing Jet Skiing Playground Open Picnic Shelter Picnic Sites Pier and Bank Fishing Walking Trail 2 Fish Feeders 5 Under Water Shelter Structures for Aquatic Life Beach Launch Area for Ski Boats and Personal Water Craft Visitors An enclosed shelter with adjacent horse shoe pits and a small basketball court are available for rentals. Located adjacent to the shelter is a small outdoor swimming pool that may be rented in combination with the shelter for an additional fee. Nearby the marina is a handicapped-accessible fishing pier. The lake is populated with bass, catfish, crappie, bream and stripers. Occasional bass fishing tournaments are held here.
