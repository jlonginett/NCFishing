Apalachia Lake (aka Apalachia Reservoir)

It is a 1,100 acre lake in the south-west corner of the state, 5 miles west of Murphy. This reservoir receives very little fishing pressure. There are 31 miles of shoreline, with very little access for shoreline fishing. A boat launch ramp is available for public use. The area is primitive, so bring everything with you. The lake has both warm water and cold water species of fish, providing a wide variety of fishing opportunities. Bass, striped bass, trout, crappie, white bass and sunfish all thrive here.
