Lake Benson

No private watercraft are allowed on Lake Benson. However there are rental boats with electric motors available at the Lake Benson Boat House. The lake covers 650 acres and has about 10 miles of shoreline. Fishing is allowed from the dock at no charge. Nice populations of bass, crappie, bream and catfish await fishing enthusiasts. This is a very picturesque setting and a great place to bring the family for a day of boating and fishing.

Lake Use Policies and Regulations:

Launching of private watercraft is prohibited. No swimming or wading is permitted anywhere on the lake. Renters must be at least 16 years of age. Alcoholic beverages are strictly prohibited. Persons consuming alcohol of any type will be denied use of the lake and adjoining city property. The possession or use of firearms is strictly prohibited. Boats are to be operated at all times in a manner that will not infringe upon the rights of other boaters, or endanger lives and property of others. No dock fishing or boat rentals during inclement or threatening weather. See Inclement Weather Protocol. N.C. Wildlife Resources Commission regulations apply while using this facility. Animals are not allowed in the water, boats, kayaks or canoes. There is a maximum of 3 people per boat. (See below for weight limits.) Boats will not be rented after 4pm. All boats must be returned to the dock at or before 5:30pm. For the safety of our patrons, we observe the weight limits posted by manufacturers. Please note these include passengers and any equipment (e.g. batteries). Jon Boats: 370 lbs Canoes: 600 lbs Tandem Kayaks: 250 lbs per seat Solo Kayaks: 250 lbs
