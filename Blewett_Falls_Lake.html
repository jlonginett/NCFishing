Blewett Falls Lake

Blewett Falls Lake is a 2,560 acre lake in the south-central part of the state, at Rockingham, NC. Blewett Falls Lake offers both bank fishing and two Wildlife Boat Landings that serve the Blewett Falls Lake side of the dam. The landing at the U.S. 74 Pee Dee River Bridge is for fishing boats and canoes only (no pleasure boating due to rocks). Average water depth behind the dam is 0-6 feet during normal weather; in front of the dam it is 15-30 feet at full water level. This 2,560-acre lake in the south-central part of the state, does not get the boat traffic like other local lakes. Bass, catfish and crappie are of primary interest here. However the reservoir also has populations of white bass, stripers and walleye. Shoreline fishing opportunities are plentiful with easy access.
