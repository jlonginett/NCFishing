Rainbow Trout

Known for its tasty pink flesh, beauty and gameness, rainbows are a favorite among the vast majority of anglers. It also appeals to the fly fisherman as it can be tempted to take properly presented flies as well as other baits and lures. It feeds on small insects, minnows, crustaceans and worms. It is ideal for both sport and dining pleasure. It prefers slightly stained to clear water with or without current. Ideal water temperature: 45° to 60°. Originally found in the Rockies and west to the Pacific ocean, it has been distributed far and wide across the US. It tends to do better in the northeast but is found in isolated areas even in the south. In waters that allow such migration the rainbow trout will remain in streams until it reaches 6" to 9" in length and then travel to lakes or oceans where it will bulk up and then return to the streams or rivers to spawn.

List of Best Lures and Bait to use:

When casting, the most common artificial lures used to catch trout are flies and spinner-type lures. Insects make up a large percentage of a trout's diet, particularly for small to medium size trout. Larger, more aggressive trout are more inclined to chase a lure imitating a minnow or other small fish.

BEST LURES:

SPINNERS

  • Effective depth: 1' to 6'
  • Recommended line test: 2# to 4#
  • Ideal conditions: Use spinners when trout are shallow and active.

CRANKBAITS

  • Effective depth: 1' to 6'
  • Recommended line test: 4# to 8#
  • Ideal conditions: Use crankbaits when trout are shallow and active.

JERKBAITS

  • Effective depth: 1' to 6'
  • Recommended line test: 4# to 8#
  • Ideal conditions: Use jerkbaits when big trout, especially browns, are shallow and active.

Trout Baits

SALMON EGGS

  • Effective depth: Any depth
  • Recommended line test: 2# to 6#
  • Ideal conditions: Use salmon eggs for all trout fishing conditions.

WORMS

  • Effective depth: Any depth
  • Recommended line test: 2# to 6#
  • Ideal conditions: Use worms, nightcrawlers or wax worms for all trout fishing conditions.

PREPARED BAITS

  • Effective depth: Any depth
  • Recommended line test: 2# to 6#
  • Ideal conditions: Use prepared baits for all trout fishing conditions.

Trout Flies

TROUT FLIES

  • Effective depth: Surface to 5'
  • Recommended line: Fly fishing leader and tippet vary with fly size and fishing conditions.
  • Ideal conditions: Use trout flies any time trout are feeding relatively shallow.

Trout anglers use canned corn, marshmallows, cheese and a host of other edibles that will stay on a small hook.
