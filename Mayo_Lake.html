Mayo Lake (aka Mayo Reservoir)

Mayo Lake is a reservoir located in northeastern Person County, North Carolina. Boating entrance, walking trails, swimming access, picnic and camp site areas (collectively known as Mayo Park) are accessed from Neals Store Road about 9 miles north of the city of Roxboro along NC 49. It is smaller than Hyco Lake located on the northwestern side of Person County. This is a 2,800-acre lake lots of shoreline, pockets, channels and docks to fish. Catfish, sunfish, pike and bowfin also call Mayo Lake their home and can add to your fishing experience. If you enjoy fishing from a kayak or canoe, this is a great place to experience. Kayak and canoe launch areas are available at Mayo Lake Park. There is a great boat launch for larger craft, a campground with RV hookups and a fishing pier. There is ample places around the lake to fish from shore.
