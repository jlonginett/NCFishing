Spotted Bass

Also known as Spots or Kentucky bass. They are easily identified by the dominant, spots along the lateral lines. There ideal water temperature is 70 to 78 degrees. The world record is 9 pounds and 8 ounces that was caught.

List of Best Lures and Bait to use:

They have a smaller mouth than the largemouth, so you can use lure sizes similar to smallmouth tackle. They feed on smaller fish as well as insects, crustaceans, frogs and worms.
